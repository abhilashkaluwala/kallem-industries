About Our Jumbo Rolls

As a leading Jumbo Rolls Manufacturer in India, Kallem Industries specializes in producing high-quality BOPP Jumbo Rolls that meet the demanding requirements of converters, packaging plants, and large-scale industrial operations. Our jumbo rolls are engineered to provide maximum efficiency, consistent performance, and superior cost-effectiveness for bulk applications.

Our BOPP Jumbo Rolls feature advanced manufacturing processes that ensure uniform thickness, consistent adhesion properties, and excellent dimensional stability. These rolls are specifically designed for high-speed converting operations and can be customized to meet your specific width, length, and performance requirements.

Technical Specifications

Comprehensive specifications for optimal performance

Physical Dimensions

  • Width Range: 500mm, 800mm, 1000mm, 1200mm, 1620mm
  • Length Options: 4000m, 5000m, 6000m, 7000m, 8000m per roll
  • Core Diameter: 76mm (3-inch) standard
  • Total Thickness: 23-50 microns
  • Film Thickness: 18-25 microns
  • Roll Weight: 200-800 kg depending on specifications

Colors & Customization

  • Standard Colors: Clear, Brown, White, Black
  • Custom Colors: Pantone matching available
  • Printing Capability: Available after slitting process
  • Specialty Finishes: Matte, Gloss, Metallic options
  • Edge Quality: Precision-cut edges for smooth converting

Performance Parameters

  • Operating Temperature: -40°C to +100°C
  • Peel Adhesion: 400-1200 g/inch (ASTM D3330)
  • Tensile Strength: 8-15 kg/25mm (ASTM D3759)
  • Elongation at Break: 180-220%
  • Shear Strength: 24+ hours at 70°C
  • Winding Tension: Consistent throughout the roll
