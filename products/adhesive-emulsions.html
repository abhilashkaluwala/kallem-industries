About Our Adhesive Emulsions

As a leading Adhesive Emulsions Manufacturer in India, Kallem Industries produces high-quality water-based adhesive solutions that meet the evolving demands of modern manufacturing industries. Our adhesive emulsions are formulated using advanced polymer technology to provide superior bonding performance while maintaining environmental responsibility and workplace safety standards.

Our adhesive emulsions feature carefully engineered polymer dispersions that deliver excellent adhesion strength, rapid drying times, and exceptional substrate compatibility. The water-based formulation ensures low VOC emissions, making them ideal for environmentally conscious manufacturing processes while providing the performance characteristics required for demanding industrial applications.

Technical Specifications

Comprehensive specifications for optimal bonding performance

Physical Properties

  • Appearance: Milky white to clear liquid emulsion
  • pH Range: 6.5 - 8.5 (neutral to slightly alkaline)
  • Viscosity: 500-2000 cps (Brookfield, 25°C)
  • Solids Content: 45-55% by weight
  • Density: 1.05-1.15 g/cm³ at 25°C
  • Particle Size: 0.1-1.0 microns average

Performance Parameters

  • Operating Temperature: 5°C to 50°C
  • Drying Time: 5-15 minutes (depending on conditions)
  • Bond Strength: 200-500 N/cm² (ASTM D1002)
  • Shear Strength: 24+ hours at 70°C
  • Freeze-Thaw Stability: 5 cycles without degradation
  • Shelf Life: 12 months in sealed containers

Packaging & Storage

  • Available Sizes: 1L, 5L, 20L, 200L containers
  • Container Type: HDPE drums, plastic cans, IBC tanks
    • Storage Conditions: Cool, dry place (5-35°C)
  • Handling: Avoid freezing and direct sunlight
  • Transportation: Non-hazardous, standard freight
  • Batch Tracking: Full traceability and quality control
