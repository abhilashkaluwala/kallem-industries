About Our PVC Tapes

As a leading PVC Tape Manufacturer in India, Kallem Industries produces high-quality polyvinyl chloride tapes that meet the demanding requirements of electrical professionals, contractors, and industrial applications. Our PVC tapes are engineered to provide superior electrical insulation, excellent weather resistance, and exceptional durability across various environmental conditions.

Our PVC tapes feature a specially formulated adhesive system that provides strong initial tack and excellent holding power. The premium PVC backing offers superior flexibility, stretchability, and resistance to UV radiation, moisture, and temperature extremes, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor electrical applications.

Why Choose Our PVC Tapes?

  • Electrical Safety: Superior electrical insulation properties meeting industry standards
  • Weather Resistance: Excellent UV, moisture, and temperature resistance for outdoor use
  • Flexibility: Superior stretchability and conformability to irregular surfaces
  • Durability: Long-lasting performance in harsh environmental conditions
  • Color Coding: Wide range of colors for easy identification and organization
  • Strong Adhesion: Reliable bonding with excellent holding power

Technical Specifications

Comprehensive specifications for electrical safety and performance

Physical Dimensions

  • Width Range: 12mm, 18mm, 24mm, 36mm, 48mm, 72mm
  • Length Options: 20m, 50m, 100m, 200m, 500m, 1000m per roll
  • Core Diameter: 25mm, 38mm, 76mm (standard)
  • Total Thickness: 23-50 microns
  • Backing Material: Premium PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride)
  • Adhesive Type: Pressure-sensitive rubber-based

Colors & Identification

  • Standard Colors: Black, White, Red, Blue, Green, Yellow, Orange
  • Custom Colors: Available on request
  • Color Coding: Industry-standard color identification
  • Opacity: High opacity for excellent visibility
  • Printing: Custom text and logo printing available
  • Surface Finish: Matte finish for easy writing

Performance Parameters

  • Operating Temperature: -20°C to +80°C
  • Peel Adhesion: 400-800 g/inch (ASTM D3330)
  • Tensile Strength: 8-12 kg/25mm (ASTM D3759)
  • Elongation at Break: 200-300%
  • Dielectric Strength: 600V/mil minimum
  • UV Resistance: Excellent outdoor durability
