About Our BOPP Tape

As a leading BOPP Tape Manufacturer in India, Kallem Industries delivers premium quality Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene tapes that set industry standards for performance and reliability. Our BOPP tapes are engineered using advanced manufacturing processes and high-grade materials to ensure exceptional adhesion, durability, and versatility across diverse applications.

Our BOPP tapes feature a unique biaxial orientation process that enhances tensile strength and dimensional stability, making them ideal for demanding packaging and industrial environments. The superior adhesive formulation ensures strong bonding to various surfaces including cardboard, plastic, metal, and glass.

  • Length Options: 50m, 100m, 200m, 500m, 1000m per roll
  • Core Diameter: 25mm, 38mm, 76mm (standard)
  • Total Thickness: 23-50 microns

Colors & Customization

  • Standard Colors: Clear, Brown, White, Black
