About Our Masking Tapes

As a leading Masking Tape Manufacturer in India, Kallem Industries produces high-quality masking tapes that meet the demanding requirements of professional painters, contractors, and industrial applications. Our masking tapes are engineered to provide precise masking, clean removal, and excellent surface protection across various substrates.

Our masking tapes feature a specially formulated adhesive that provides strong initial tack while ensuring clean, residue-free removal. The high-quality crepe paper backing offers excellent conformability to irregular surfaces and superior tear resistance for easy hand-tearing applications.
