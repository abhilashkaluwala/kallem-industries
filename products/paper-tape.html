About Our Paper Tapes

As a leading Paper Tape Manufacturer in India, Kallem Industries produces high-quality kraft paper tapes that meet the growing demand for sustainable packaging solutions. Our paper tapes are crafted from premium kraft paper with specially formulated adhesives, offering excellent sealing performance while maintaining environmental responsibility.

Our paper tapes feature superior tensile strength and excellent adhesion properties, making them ideal for carton sealing, package labeling, and various industrial applications. The natural kraft paper backing provides excellent printability for custom branding and identification requirements.

Why Choose Our Paper Tapes?

  • Environmental Responsibility: 100% recyclable and biodegradable kraft paper backing

  • Printability: Excellent surface for custom printing and branding
  • Versatility: Suitable for various packaging materials and surfaces
  • Cost-Effective: Competitive pricing for sustainable packaging solutions
  • Customization: Available in various widths, colors, and custom printing options

Technical Specifications

Comprehensive specifications for sustainable performance

Physical Dimensions

  • Width Range: 12mm, 18mm, 24mm, 36mm, 48mm, 72mm
  • Length Options: 50m, 100m, 200m, 500m, 1000m per roll
  • Core Diameter: 25mm, 38mm, 76mm (standard)
  • Total Thickness: 23-50 microns
  • Backing Material: Premium kraft paper
  • Adhesive Type: Pressure-sensitive rubber-based

Colors & Customization

  • Standard Colors: Brown, White, Natural, Beige
  • Custom Colors: Available on request
  • Printing Capability: High-quality custom printing
  • Surface Finish: Matte finish for excellent printability
  • Opacity: High opacity for better visibility
  • Branding: Custom logos and text printing

Performance Parameters

  • Operating Temperature: -20°C to +80°C
  • Peel Adhesion: 400-800 g/inch (ASTM D3330)
  • Tensile Strength: 8-12 kg/25mm (ASTM D3759)
  • Elongation at Break: 10-20%
  • Shear Strength: 8+ hours at 70°C
  • Moisture Resistance: Good resistance to humidity
