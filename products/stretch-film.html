About Our Stretch Films

As a leading Stretch Film Manufacturer in India, Kallem Industries produces high-quality polyethylene stretch films that meet the demanding requirements of modern logistics, warehousing, and industrial packaging operations. Our stretch films are engineered to provide superior stretch performance, excellent cling properties, and exceptional durability for securing and protecting goods during storage and transportation.

Our stretch films feature advanced polymer technology that delivers optimal stretch ratios while maintaining excellent puncture and tear resistance. The specially formulated cling additives ensure secure wrapping and load stability, making them ideal for both manual and automated palletizing applications across various industries.

Why Choose Our Stretch Films?

  • Superior Stretchability:High elongation for secure pallet wrapping
  • Puncture Resistant:Protects goods from tears and transit damage
  • Cling Performance:Excellent self-adhering properties for stability
  • Clarity & Transparency:Easy product identification and scanning
  • Lightweight:Reduces shipping costs and material waste
  • Versatile Use:Suitable for hand and machine applications

Comprehensive specifications for optimal packaging performance

Physical Dimensions

  • Width Range: 200mm, 300mm, 400mm, 500mm
  • Length Options: 1000m, 1500m, 2000m, 3000m per roll
  • Core Diameter: 76mm (standard)
  • Film Thickness: 12-25 microns
  • Material: Premium LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene)
  • Roll Weight: 5kg - 15kg per roll

Colors & Transparency

  • Standard Colors: Clear, Black, White, Blue
  • Custom Colors: Available on request
  • Transparency: High clarity for product visibility
  • UV Protection: Available with UV inhibitors
  • Printing: Custom branding and text printing
  • Opacity Levels: Various opacity options available

Performance Parameters

  • Operating Temperature: -20°C to +80°C
  • Stretch Ratio: 200-300% (ASTM D4649)
  • Tensile Strength: 15-25 kg/25mm (ASTM D882)
  • Elongation at Break: 400-600%
  • Puncture Resistance: 200-400 N (ASTM D1709)
  • Tear Resistance: 50-100 N (ASTM D1922)
