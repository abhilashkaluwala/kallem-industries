About Our Advertisement Tapes

As a leading Advertisement Tape Manufacturer in India, Kallem Industries specializes in creating high-impact promotional tapes that elevate your brand presence and marketing campaigns. Our advertisement tapes combine superior printing technology with durable adhesive properties to deliver exceptional results across diverse applications.

Our custom printed tapes feature advanced flexographic printing processes that ensure vibrant colors, crisp text, and long-lasting visual appeal. The high-quality BOPP film base provides excellent durability, weather resistance, and strong adhesion, making these tapes perfect for both indoor and outdoor promotional activities.

Technical Specifications

Comprehensive specifications for optimal performance

Physical Dimensions

  • Width Range: 12mm, 18mm, 24mm, 36mm, 48mm, 72mm
  • Length Options: 50m, 100m, 200m, 500m, 1000m per roll
  • Core Diameter: 25mm, 38mm, 76mm (standard)
  • Total Thickness: 23-50 microns
  • Film Thickness: 18-25 microns
  • Print Area: Up to 90% of tape width

Printing & Customization

  • Printing Process: High-resolution flexographic printing
  • Color Capability: Up to 6 colors with registration
  • Base Colors: White, Clear, Brown, Black, Custom
  • Design Options: Logos, text, graphics, QR codes
  • Specialty Finishes: Matte, Gloss, Metallic, Holographic
  • Minimum Order: Custom quantities available

Performance Parameters

  • Operating Temperature: -40°C to +100°C
  • Peel Adhesion: 400-1200 g/inch (ASTM D3330)
  • Tensile Strength: 8-15 kg/25mm (ASTM D3759)
  • Elongation at Break: 180-220%
  • UV Resistance: Excellent outdoor durability
  • Moisture Resistance: Water-resistant printing inks
