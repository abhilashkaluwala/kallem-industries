30+ Years of Experience

Building a Brighter Future.

Kallem Industries, which has been around for over 30 years, was founded in 1996 and serves the packaging industry. We produce and export all varieties of industrial tapes, including printed tapes, lane marking tapes, and Bopp tapes self-adhesive tapes. Our business continues to expand through innovation, creativity, and customisation for videos. At Kallem Industries, we have a passion for anything that binds, sticks, adheres, or whatever else you can think of!

Our quality Bopp tapes feature attributes like non-toxicity, colorlessness, safety, ease of use, high efficiency, high anti-buffering strength, good retraction rate, anti-piercing, good tear resistance, and so forth. They are designed to operate well even under adverse weather situations.
